<p><strong>The Lychee Honey Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extracted Honey</li><li>Pressed Honey</li><li>Comb Honey</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Lychee Honey market can be categorized into three main types: extracted honey, pressed honey, and comb honey. Extracted honey involves extracting nectar from the honeycomb using mechanical means, resulting in a clear, smooth product. Pressed honey is obtained by applying pressure to honeycombs, allowing the liquid to seep out while retaining some natural particles. Comb honey consists of honey still encased in its wax comb, prized for its texture and flavor, and is often sold directly as a natural product.</p></p>

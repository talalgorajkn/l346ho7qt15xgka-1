<p><strong>The Mist Eliminator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wire-mesh Mist Eliminators</li><li>Baffle-type Mist Climinators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mist eliminator market comprises various types, primarily wire-mesh and baffle-type mist eliminators. Wire-mesh mist eliminators utilize interwoven metal fibers to capture droplets, ensuring efficient liquid removal from gas streams. They are effective for moderate gas velocities. Baffle-type mist eliminators employ a series of baffles to redirect gas flow, causing droplets to coalesce and fall back into the process. This design is suited for higher gas velocities and larger droplet sizes, enhancing separation efficiency in industrial applications.</p></p>

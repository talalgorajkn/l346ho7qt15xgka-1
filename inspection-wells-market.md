<p><p>The Inspection Wells market comprises two primary types: Rotational Molding Inspection Wells and Welded Piping Inspection Wells. Rotational Molding Inspection Wells are produced using a molding process that allows for complex shapes and seamless construction, enhancing durability and corrosion resistance, making them suitable for various applications.
